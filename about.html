We believe in healing through art. We believe that the revolution begins and ends with art. We are a radical, inclusive, art zine based in Sheffield that mostly expresses itself through poetry. We are like skin because we break, bruise, bleed, heal, scar and feel.

Featured below and on the main page are the issues we have published so far. Our inspiration page features other poetry and artistic mediums which inspire us. Our events page is where we coordinate with other artistic ventures happening around the city. If you have any ideas or feedback, please contact us or mention us on social media! Lastly, if you would like to submit a poem or a piece of art, please read the submission guidelines and fill out the form below.
